Gallery

This is our collection of fantastic species, which are currently under investigation. Each one of these interpretations is hand-rendered by our top field researchers. Learn more about them with us!

Lion Shark

The newest species in our research facility. Observed in two variants, we hope this will give us insight into the other genetic anomalies that have been discovered.

Jackelope

Despite contrary belief, we have actually found colonies of this creature near the lion shark habitat. They are not friendly, but amiable enough with the researchers.

Quilled Platypus

Not to be mistaken for its monotreme cousin the Echidna, the Quilled Platypus possesses an extreme temperment in terms of self defense, and will actively seek to harm both platypus and echidna neighbors. We are evaluating for invasiveness.

Turtle Dove

A unique bird with a carapace cartilage sheild. We are still investigating as to how it is capable of flight, despite its bodyweight ratio.

Spider Monkey

A member of the mammilian primate family with an advanced grip and bipedal walking capabilities. It has the above average number of arms and legs of a typical mammal, however, and is also venomous.

Aquatic Horse

This majestic creature ranges from about 2 to 35 cm tall. The majority of their diet seems to be brine shrimp and plankton. They can be found near Lion Shark habitats.

Infinity Snake

This curious creature appears to be a normal snake, but has a strange affinity to biting it's tail. It seems to be entirely healthy, but does not consume the food added to its cage. Live prey somehow ends up expired, but untouched, and we can't figure out how to make it let go of the tail.

Wolf Man

This creature is no monster, but simply Paul the Office Consultant. He transforms every once and a while with no distinctive prompting, and he agreed to live in our facility while we research the cause of his situation. He's very good at cards.

Unnamed

This animal is by far the most difficult we have ever worked with. It seems capable of above average thinking capacity and makes use of both symbolism and multiple levels of communication. To be honest, we're not quite sure what we've found, but it seems restless whenever we try to investigate the Infinity Snake...
